The Grand Hotel

The Grand Hotel offers modern rooms with free WiFi access. Guests can enjoy a swim at the outdoor pool or a pampering treatment at the spa. Each air-conditioned room features a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a sofa seating area and a private balcony. Amenities include a safety deposit box, a minibar and tea/coffee making facilities. Offering a bath or shower, the en suite bathroom comes with free toiletries and a hairdryer. Children can take part in the activities at the kid's club and the fitness centre can be used to stay active. Other facilities and services at this hotel include a 24-hour front desk, a free shuttle service and meeting facilities. Free parking is available. Couples particularly like the location.
